# `cl-availability`

Display an availability message with localized delivery lead time and cost.

The `cl-availability` component is the owner of the availability information.
It has the responsibility to fetch the inventory of the provided code and dispatch this information to its [**cl-availability-status**](?path=/docs/components-availability-cl-availability-status--basic) and [**cl-availability-info**](?path=/docs/components-availability-cl-availability-info--basic) children.
